Guidelines

  1. YARA can only be used in events using the solution

  2. Do not use terms like "Powered by", "Hosted on" etc while using YARA

  3. Do not use YARA in a way that implies partnership, sponsorship or endorsement

  4. YARA brand assets should not be modified in any way, such as changing the design or color

  5. Do not use any other logo or icons to represent YARA. Only use brand assets that are downloadable from the YARA Dashboard.

  6. You can scale the YARA logo depending on the screen/poster size. The aspect ratio must be maintained so as to ensure that the logo is not stretched.

  7. Do not use any color other than the mentioned to represent the YARA logo.

  8. Do not rotate, tilt or change the direction of the logo

  9. Use the latest version of the logo available on YARA website. In case you are using an older version of the logo make sure to update to the latest version.

  10. Do not combine any part of YARA logo with your name, marks or generic terms.

  11. Incase of using YARA logo on a poster/screen, importance should be given to Organisers'logo. YARA logo should not occupy more than 20% of the total screen /poster space.

  12. YARA is a registered trademark of Voris Systems Pvt Ltd.

At first use of YARA dashboard, any person might feel that a lot restrictions are placed on the content and images that can be uploaded. Naturally, the question will arise as to why we adhere to such strict guidelines. We do understand your concerns, but let us assure you that following the guidelines will make sure the users have a memorable experience using the Yara. While designing Yara, we have taken into consideration the views and opinions of all the stakeholders involved in Event Management industry be it the Organisers, Sponsors, Exhibitors and the Delegates. Design was done is such a way that the user will never for a moment get bored using Yara. And the app that you see today is the culmination of that effort. It has got the right mix of what each group desires. We have also made sure that interests of one group (e.g: Organiser) does not overlap that of the other (e.g.: Delegates) and vice versa. It is in this regard we have come up with such restrictions. A quality app will enhance the overall experience of the Event.

Advertisement

Sponsors are the key to any event's success and we realise their importance. User experience and Advertisements usually don't go hand in hand. Too many Ads leads to a nightmarish User experience and too less Ads would mean less revenue for the Organisers. The success lies in finding the right balance. So we have come up with AdSpace which is a tailor made Advertisement solution from Yara. It's an easy to use solution. Once you have purchased the desired Ad package, all you need to do is to upload the images or videos. Our Smart Delivery system makes sure that the Ads will be displayed to the users at the appropriate time. We have made sure that the Pulse cannot be flooded with Ads as this will compromise the User Experience.

The AdSpace feature also comes with inbuilt analytics. So you can get to know the effectiveness of each Ad campaign (no:of views, overall interaction to name a few) AdSpace is the only medium to post Ads on Yara. Organisers should make use of AdSpace in order of post Ads. Yara reserves the right to take Ads posted outside of AdSpace.

Vote

Organisers can put to Vote questions ensuring better interaction with the delegates. A few facts to keep in mind while creating Vote are mentioned below.

  • Questions should be relevant to the event being held
  • Feedback questions cannot put to Vote (Feedback regarding sessions, food, venue, etc).
  • Questions must be clear and concise


The results of the Vote will be visible to the delegates once he/she chooses to vote. Asking for feedback using Vote will give away the feedback to the delegates. So it is not at all recommend to use Vote to gather feedback. Our automated feedback system will ensure you get the right feedback from the delegates.

Feedback

There are multiple options for Organisers to get real time feedback from the Delegates. Yara InSights provide live feedback about the various sessions, speakers, exhibitors and sponsors. Along with the InSights, at the end of each day/event Yara's feedback system puts forth a pre defined set of Feedback questions. We do ensure that these feedback queries will be presented in a very user friendly manner, thereby increasing the chances of genuine feedback from the Delegates. As we had mentioned earlier, Yara will handle the feedback process automatically. Organisers should not use any other medium to get user feedback.

We have put a lot of effort and thought in the design and quality of Yara. As a result of this, you have access to an app that has unparalleled quality. We expect our users to also maintain the same quality standards. Thus we have introduced certain criteria for the images and videos that are to be uploaded.

Make use of our "Smart Delivery System"

We have come up with our unique system called "Smart Delivery". The system which allows the Organisers to upload the images/videos/text. Upon doing so, the system ensures that the Images, videos and text are delivered at the right time to the delegates thereby increasing its effectiveness. Smart Delivery option can be used for various features like AdSpace, Vote, Coupons and ShareBox. Smart Delivery system works on an algorithm which ensures that the desired content is pushed to the users at the right time without spoiling the user experience.

Of course, the organisers can push content anytime they choose to. But we strongly recommend using Smart Delivery which in turn will help you in cutting down time and effort. You no longer need to run around thinking what is the best time to post an Ad. Our Smart Delivery has it covered.

Event name and date

Most events are planned very well in advance. Hence any changes to Name and date are extremely rare. We do not allow organisers to edit the Event name and date once the Event has been created. Despite this, if an Organiser wishes to change the Event name and date, they can raise a ticket regarding the same using HappyHelp and our Support team will review and make the necessary changes.

Free Events

At the outset, we would like to make it very clear that only certain organisations which fall in the following categories NGOs, Non Profits, Charity are eligible to apply for free event. Applications from organisations outside the specified list will not be entertained. Relevant organisations can contact Yara Team directly mentioning the complete details of the proposed event. Each application will be reviewed on a case to case basis. Yara holds the right to accept/reject an application based on aforementioned criteria. If an application is accepted, a YaraKey will be sent directly to the Organiser Inbox. The YaraKey will be applicable only for that particular event and cannot be redeemed for any other event.

Content Guidelines

Organisers must ensure that content containing sexually explicit material, violence, bullying, hate speech based on race, religion, disability, gender, age, nationality, sexual orientation etc must not find its way to the user pulse. Utmost care must be taken while uploading content in the dashboard. Voris hold the right to take down any content that does not meet the above mentioned criteria.
